Q & A
Besides dance and choreography, what's other parts of production that impressed you?
Oh man, now I know what's behind the art scene. Costume design, lighting design, sound editing, rehearsal scheduling, communication with dancers and all parts of the team...
I were seriously a project manager, in an insanely tight deadline. But I sincerely loved the journey to bring an idea into reality.
What's a moment to remember from this project?
At a late night rehearsal, all of us struggled to catch a timing. We almost gave up. The moment the dancers get it, we all yelled, jumped and run to hug each other.
There were lots of laughs, funny faces, and good conversations in our journey.
